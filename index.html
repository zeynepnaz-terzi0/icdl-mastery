What is ICDL?

ICDL (International Computer Driving Licence) is the world's leading computer skills certification programme. It validates your digital competency across 7 modules — from computer basics to cybersecurity and online collaboration — and is recognised by employers and universities worldwide.

📍 Based on Bocconi University ICDL exam guidelines
📋

The 7 ICDL Modules

Candidates must pass all 7 modules to earn the full ICDL certification. Completing ICDL fulfils a mandatory prerequisite for several Bocconi courses including Computer Science (30424), Legal Tech (30625), and Computer Skills for Economics (30068/6033).

Purchase a Skills Card & Exams

All purchases are made exclusively through the online payment form in your yoU@B Diary. Bocconi students benefit from discounted pricing:

  • ICDL Skills Card: €60 (records all passed exams, no expiry)
  • Individual exam module: €18 each

The virtual Skills Card is sent to your Bocconi email within 2 working days after payment is processed.

📝

Exam Registration Procedure

After purchasing, register through your yoU@B Diary → IT Certifications → "Registration for ICDL exams at BOCCONI". Key rules:

  • Enter your 7-digit Skills Card number on first access
  • Maximum 3 exams per session
  • Choose from available dates within the next 4 weeks
  • Modifications/cancellations allowed until 23:59 on Thursday before exam week
  • Confirmation with time & location arrives the Friday before your exam
📊

Check Exam Results

Results are available through the private area on the AICA website at www.aicanet.it. Steps:

  • Register as a candidate on the AICA website
  • Select ICDL from the certification menu
  • Enter your Skills Card number (without the "IT-" prefix)

For access issues, use the online contact form on the AICA website for technical support.

🏆

Receive Your ICDL Certificate

Once you have successfully passed all 7 modules, the IT Education Center will email your ICDL certificate in PDF format to your Bocconi email address.

  • Certificate arrives approximately 1 month after completing all modules
  • No printing required for curricular exam registration
  • The PDF certificate is the official valid document
